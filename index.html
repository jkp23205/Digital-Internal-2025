In the Bible, there are several influencial and significant people. Mary, mother of Jesus, Peter, John the Evangelist, and even God himself. However, these three figures not only had a direct impact of Jesus' life, but the shaping and delivery of the Bible's message itself. Who better to learn about, than Jesus' betrayer, the first witness to His ressurection, and a king who had a thousand consorts.
